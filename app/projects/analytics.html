Analytics

Landing page for data management and analytics platform
Background

Part of Metaps app data and analytics platform, the focus of this landing page was purely data-orientated. Geometric shapes interact with particles and grids denote a mathematical consensus. On the development side, two separate sliders reduce the bulk of the page, allowing the user to view the dashboard UI.

Involvement

Art direction, branding, UI design, front-end development

Recruitment Page

Landing page for recruitment at Metaps Inc.
Background

A recruitment page for Metaps, the design focuses on their business goals and staff. Brightly contrasting sections of content with icons attune your eye to each facet of information. A responsive slider displays each employee introduction keeping the page bulk to a minimum. My photography tries to capture a unique characteristic of each employee.

Involvement

Art direction, photography, UI design, front-end development
